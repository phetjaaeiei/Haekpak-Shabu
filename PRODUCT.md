# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Thai diners, students, workers, friends, and families deciding where to eat shabu. They are likely browsing on mobile, checking the food mood, scanning the menu, and opening maps when they are ready to visit.

## Product Purpose

Haekpak Shabu is a buffet shabu restaurant landing page that makes the food feel vivid and immediate, gives visitors enough menu confidence to choose the restaurant, and moves ready diners to Google Maps without friction.

## Brand Personality

Energetic, hospitable, appetizing. The voice should feel warm, lively, and confident, with the sensory pull of a busy shabu table at night.

## Anti-references

Avoid generic SaaS landing page structure, washed-out beige food pages, repeated identical icon cards, hidden or low-contrast food photography, decorative glass panels, and template-like sections that make the restaurant feel anonymous.

## Design Principles

Lead with appetite: every major fold should make the food visible, warm, and close.

Make the route obvious: visitors should always know how to view the menu or open the map.

Use energy with control: strong color and contrast should create heat without sacrificing readability.

Break the card rhythm: menu content should feel curated and browsable, not like a generated grid.

Keep the Thai dining context clear: copy, hierarchy, and mobile behavior should fit quick restaurant decisions.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

Target WCAG AA contrast for text and controls. Support reduced motion. Keep touch targets comfortable on mobile. Do not rely on color alone for key actions or category meaning.
